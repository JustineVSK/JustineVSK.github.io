Willemijn.

For this experiment we used again plastic. We wrapped it around the body, which made it difficult to move. It gives a feeling of being trapped in the material. Fear is setting in because it isn’t possible to get out the plastic anymore. Because the plastic is very tightly wrapped, It becomes difficult to breath. While craving for air, there is a desperate feeling that nobody can help you. The feeling of loneliness takes over while struggling for getting out of the tightly wrapped plastic cocoon.
