Luna.

We felt inspired by the fact that Luna described she could be different persons, loud and funny in the morning and silent in the evening, like she was playing in a puppet show. We decided that we wanted to work with rope for this experiment. We tied the hands up and looked for different ways of implementing light. We wanted the outcome to be quite theatrical and used backlight to enhance the dramatic effect
