Virginie. 

Virginie also describes depression being something physical. She speaks about the mind being important, but the body even more. Her story starts with the sentence “drowning in your own fears until you lose any vitality” We felt that these words were very powerful and had great depth to it. She also added an image showing a woman drowning, which had for her two meanings: it could be seen as drowning or emerging out of the water and coming up for air. In the experiment we wanted to put the human body again in focus but only showing parts of it. The rest is hidden in the turbid water, it symbolizes that depression is often very hidden for the outside world. We left it undecided if the body is drowning or emerging.
