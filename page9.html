Olga.

In Olga’s story we read about an intense fear and a restless feeling that she can’t get rid of, it follows her around. She is trying to work with her whole body to push negative thoughts away and she is anxiously trying to find peace of mind. We made a series of portraits, a woman desperately trying to get way of her unrest. We wanted to express that she has a lot of anxiety and it is visually deforming her face. The picture becomes ugly, it almost looks that the depression is becoming a demon and is taking over her personality.
