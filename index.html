D E P R E S S I O N

'A mental condition characterized by feelings
of severe despondency and dejection.'

For the casestudy Multidisciplinarity we were free to choose our own subject. We started with a brainstorm session, which eventually led us to the topic Depression. In the beginning we were quite lost with this subject and we decided to ask friends and family which were once, or still are at the moment, suffering from depression to put their thoughts on paper. With no clear result in mind, their personal stories guided us through the process. What we learned is that depression is not static, and that it is next to a mental pain something very physical. Therefore we put the human body into focus to emphasize the physical suffering.
