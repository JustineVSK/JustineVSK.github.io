Julia I.

The first experiment is showing a person covered in plastic, we wanted to show a inner and outer world, as Julia talks about feeling locked in and she can see the outside world but is not really part of it. We used plastic because we wanted to make it an uncomfortable and suffocating experience, with the intent that she has to come up for air afterwards. The shadows were also something that we found important: it represents light and darkness.
