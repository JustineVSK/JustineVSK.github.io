Behind the scenes

Looking for a location

Options

During our work we realized that we needed a special location for the presentation. Our work and researches needed to have a connection with the space. Since we worked in the Temple building for our first experiment, we felt that the high ceilings and the emptiness relates to the drawings we created. The process of the experiments developed a fulfillment in the Temple. Unfortunately the room was not available at the time for the jury and we searched in the whole school for another suitable room. Another location where we could imagine the presentation was in the attic of another building but it wasn’t either accessible. We found the cinema room that has a dark and a light area, which transfers our topic as well. The dark area is capable to show our movie and it is even beneficial to work with the drawings.
